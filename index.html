    What is climate change?

    How is climate change affecting us?

    Why is it important to help?

    First, you may be wondering, what even is climate change? According to Merriam Webster, it is a "significant and long-lasting change in the Earth's climate and weather patterns". To add, it is usually associated with global warming, and indicates increasing temperature on Earth, mainly due to the greenhouse effect, where greenhouse gases are released into the atmosphere, generating lots of heat.

    Climate change causes severe, serious storms, drought, and heat waves that can prevent us from our daily activities. It's harming the Earth with rising sea levels, higher ocean temperature, and melting of glaciers. These can result in severe direct consequences for animals and their habitats, and cause disaster for people's lives and the communities they're part of. Not only is climate change affecting us, but it's affecting so many different other ecosystems and organisms on Earth.

    Climate change is something that affects everyone and our everyday life. To keep this world safe and habitable for future generations as well as ourselves, we must fight to stop this change. There are countless ways to help!
