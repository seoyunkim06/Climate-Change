    Climative: Initiative to Educate & Inform about Climate Change.

    Learn about the Climative mission:

    Climative was founded by a group of high school scholars from Kode With Klossy 2022 camp who wanted to shed light on climate change and the importance of people's roles in helping prevent it. This website was created to help others learn about the seriousness of the problem, and learn that there are various different ways to help.
